Works Cited for Team Content

Editor's Note: *While standard practice would be to list all sources through footnotes on each topic page, there was a heavy amount of variation across different groups in attributing sources. Some groups also did not specify which page a source should be attributed to.

To ensure consistency in attributing sources for team content across the website, I made the decision to list the works cited from each team on a separate page. I also specified what page a source was from, if that information was available to me.

Since almost every attribution was just a website link, I made edits to display more descriptive link text, citing the title of the webpage and the creator of the content. If the original team member had their own link text (as seen across the citations for the 'Best Practices, Tips, and Examples' page of the Visual Transition Effects chapter), I did not make any edits.*
